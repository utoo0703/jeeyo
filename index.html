About Jeeyo

Your Trusted Partner in Senior Care

At Jeeyo, we believe that our elders deserve to live with dignity, independence, and joy. Our mission is to provide comprehensive assistance services that enable senior citizens to Live Free, Live Long in the comfort of their own homes.

We understand the challenges that come with aging, and our dedicated team is committed to making everyday tasks easier, ensuring peace of mind for both seniors and their families.

What We Do: Jeeyo acts as your trusted bridge to connect you with quality service providers. We help you avail various services by coordinating with verified third-party providers, handling all the logistics, bookings, and coordination on your behalf.

From grocery shopping to doctor appointments, from appliance repairs to legal assistance, we're here to help with every aspect of daily life that might become challenging with age.
